Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

We are open Tuesday through Saturday from 7am to 3pm.

Do you offer catering or special orders?

Yes, we do catering for events large and small. Contact us to discuss your catering needs.

How far in advance should I place a catering order?

For most catering orders, 2-3 weeks notice is recommended. However, please contact us to check availability for your event date.

Do you offer discounts for large orders?

Yes, for orders of 5 dozen or more items we offer a 10% discount. Just mention you need a volume discount when ordering.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, credit cards, and PayPal. For catering or large online orders, invoice options may also be available through request.

How can I stay up-to-date on specials and events?

Follow us on Facebook and Instagram, or sign up for our email newsletter on our homepage!
