MY HOME TOWN

My hometown is Tanga, which is among Tanzanian's administrative regions covering over an area of 26,667 km2 (10,296 sq mi)

The regional capital is the municipality of Tanga city, located in northeast Tanzania. The region is bordered by Kenya and Kilimanjaro Region to the north; Manyara Region to the west; and Morogoro and Pwani Regions to the south

It has a coastline to the east with the Indian Ocean

In my homeland there is Fertile soil that support agricultural activities

According to the 2022 national census, the region had a population of 2,615,597
